FEBRUARY 6, 2024

Paving the way for Ph research: DOST-ASTI's PREGINET connects institutions with eduroam

eduroam (education roaming) is a global roaming access service developed for the international research and education community. DOST-ASTI, through PREGINET, was the first to deploy eduroam services in the Philippines in the early 2000s. The service provides reliable and convenient wireless internet access to students, researchers, and staff at participating institutions.
