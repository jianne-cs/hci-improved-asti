RESEARCH DATA

// OVERVIEW

ASTI manages comprehensive data infrastructure for Earth observation, environmental monitoring, and scientific research. Our data systems support evidence-based decision-making for disaster risk reduction, climate adaptation, agricultural planning, and environmental conservation.

We adhere to open data principles and international standards to ensure data quality, accessibility, and interoperability.

// PEDRO CENTER

The Philippine Earth Data Resource Observation (PEDRO) Center is ASTI's facility for satellite data reception, processing, and distribution. Established to support the Philippines' space program, PEDRO provides timely and reliable satellite imagery for various applications.

  • Data Reception: S-band and X-band receiving systems for multiple satellite missions
  • Image Processing: Geometric correction, radiometric calibration, and atmospheric correction
  • Value-Added Products: Land cover maps, vegetation indices, flood extent maps
  • Data Archiving: Long-term storage of satellite imagery spanning decades
  • Distribution: Online portal and API access for data users

// PHILSENSORS NETWORK

The PhilSensors Network is a nationwide system of environmental sensors providing real-time data for monitoring and early warning. The network supports government agencies, researchers, and communities in understanding environmental conditions across the archipelago.

  • Air Quality Monitoring: PM2.5, PM10, NO2, O3, CO measurements
  • Water Level Monitoring: River and coastal water level sensors
  • Weather Stations: Temperature, humidity, rainfall, wind speed/direction
  • Seismic Stations: Accelerometers for earthquake monitoring
  • Solar Radiation: Pyranometers for solar energy assessment
