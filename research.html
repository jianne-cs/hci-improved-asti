Explore our technology domains and research programs

Space Technology

ASTI leads the Philippines' space technology initiatives, including satellite development, ground station operations, and satellite data applications for disaster management, agriculture, and environmental monitoring.

Key Projects: DIWATA Microsatellite Program, STAMINA4Space, Satellite Ground Station Operations

LEARN MORE

Wireless Technology

Research and development in wireless communications including 5G technologies, rural broadband connectivity, and IoT communication protocols for connecting underserved communities.

Key Projects: 5G Testbed, Rural Wireless Broadband, LoRaWAN Deployment

LEARN MORE

Artificial Intelligence

Developing AI solutions for various sectors including healthcare, agriculture, disaster response, and public services. Focus on responsible and ethical AI development.

Key Projects: AI for Agriculture, Natural Language Processing for Filipino, Computer Vision for Disaster Assessment

LEARN MORE

Emerging Technologies

Research on cutting-edge technologies such as quantum computing applications, blockchain for government services, and advanced materials for electronics.

Key Projects: Quantum Computing Research, Blockchain for Supply Chain, Advanced Materials Development

LEARN MORE

Robotics

Development of robotic systems for agriculture, underwater exploration, and industrial automation to address national challenges in productivity and resource management.

Key Projects: Agricultural Drones, Underwater ROVs, Autonomous Systems Lab

LEARN MORE

Research Data

PEDRO Sensors and PhilSensors networks provide real-time environmental monitoring across the Philippines for weather, air quality, and seismic data collection.

Key Projects: PhilSensors Network, PEDRO Data Platform, Open Data Initiative
