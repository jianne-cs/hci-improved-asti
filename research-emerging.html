EMERGING TECHNOLOGIES

// OVERVIEW

ASTI's Emerging Technologies research explores cutting-edge technologies with high potential for future applications. We monitor global technology trends, evaluate their relevance to Philippine contexts, and develop capabilities to harness these technologies for national development.

Our work ensures the Philippines remains competitive in the global technology landscape while addressing unique local challenges and opportunities.

// KEY RESEARCH AREAS

  • Quantum Computing - Research on quantum computing applications, post-quantum cryptography, and quantum-ready infrastructure planning
  • Blockchain and Distributed Ledger Technology - Applications for secure government transactions, supply chain verification, and digital identity
  • Advanced Materials - Research on electronic materials, sensors, and nanomaterials for various applications
  • Internet of Things (IoT) - Smart systems for agriculture, cities, and infrastructure management
  • AR/VR/XR - Immersive technologies for education, training, and visualization
  • Digital Twin Technology - Virtual replicas of physical systems for simulation and optimization

// CURRENT INITIATIVES

  • Post-Quantum Cryptography Research - Preparing government systems for quantum computing threats
  • Blockchain for Supply Chain - Pilot projects for agricultural product tracing and pharmaceutical supply chain
  • Smart Cities Testbed - IoT deployments in selected cities for urban management solutions
  • XR Training Platform - Virtual reality for skills training in various sectors

// FUTURE ROADMAPS

We develop technology roadmaps to guide long-term research and development:

  • Quantum Computing Readiness Framework
  • Blockchain Implementation Guidelines for Government
  • IoT Philippines Strategy 2030
