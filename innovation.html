INNOVATION SYSTEMS

ASTI operates three major innovation systems that support research, education, and technological development across the Philippines.

COARE

Computing and Archiving Research Environment

COARE is a high-performance computing (HPC) facility that provides computational resources and storage solutions for researchers across the Philippines. It supports data-intensive research in climate science, genomics, physics, and other fields requiring massive computational power.

Services: HPC cluster access, cloud computing, data archiving, scientific software support, training and workshops

Current Capacity: Multi-node cluster with GPU acceleration, petabyte-scale storage

ACCESS COARE PORTAL

PREGINET

Philippine Research, Education and Government Information Network

PREGINET is the national research and education network (NREN) of the Philippines. It provides high-speed broadband connectivity linking academic institutions, research centers, and government agencies for collaborative research and knowledge sharing.

Services: High-speed interconnectivity, video conferencing, distributed computing, network monitoring, eduroam access

Coverage: 100+ member institutions nationwide

VISIT PREGINET

EPDC

Electronics Product Development Center

EPDC supports the development of electronics products and systems through prototyping, testing, and design services. It provides Filipino inventors and entrepreneurs access to facilities and expertise for electronics innovation.

Services: PCB design and fabrication, component sourcing, prototyping, testing and certification support, technology consultation

Focus Areas: IoT devices, consumer electronics, industrial automation, medical devices
