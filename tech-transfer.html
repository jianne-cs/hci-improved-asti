TECHNOLOGY TRANSFER

// COMMERCIALIZATION

ASTI's Technology Transfer Unit facilitates the commercialization of research outputs and innovations developed by our researchers and partners. We bridge the gap between research and market application.

Our technology transfer programs aim to:

  • • Promote the adoption of ASTI-developed technologies by industry
  • • Protect and manage intellectual property generated from research
  • • Facilitate licensing agreements and technology partnerships
  • • Support spin-off companies and startups based on ASTI technologies

// TECHNOLOGY TRANSFER PROCESS

4

// PARTNERSHIPS

We collaborate with industry partners, academic institutions, and government agencies to accelerate technology adoption and innovation diffusion. Our partners include major Filipino corporations, SMEs, and international technology firms.

// IP MANAGEMENT

Our Intellectual Property Office manages patents, copyrights, and other proprietary assets developed through our research programs. We ensure that innovations are properly protected and utilized for national benefit.
