ROBOTICS

// OVERVIEW

ASTI's Robotics research program develops robotic systems for industrial and service applications. Our work addresses national challenges in agriculture, infrastructure inspection, environmental monitoring, and healthcare through the development of autonomous and semi-autonomous robotic systems.

We focus on practical applications that can be deployed by Filipino communities and industries, with emphasis on affordability, reliability, and ease of use.

// KEY PROJECTS

  • AgriBot: Agricultural Robotics - Autonomous tractors, robotic harvesters, and crop monitoring drones for precision agriculture. Addresses labor shortages and improves farm productivity.
  • DeepEye: Underwater ROV - Remotely Operated Vehicle for coral reef monitoring, underwater infrastructure inspection, and marine research. Capable of depths up to 300 meters.
  • BridgeBot: Infrastructure Inspector - Autonomous robot for bridge and tunnel inspection, using LiDAR and ultrasonic sensors for structural health monitoring.
  • CARE: Assistive Robots - Service robots for elderly care and healthcare assistance, including medication reminders and mobility support.
  • Disaster Response Robots - Search and rescue robots for earthquake and typhoon response operations.
