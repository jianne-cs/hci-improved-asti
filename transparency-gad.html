GENDER AND DEVELOPMENT (GAD)

// ABOUT GAD

The Gender and Development (GAD) Program of ASTI is committed to promoting gender equality, equity, and women's empowerment in the workplace and in the conduct of science and technology research. ASTI adheres to the Philippine Magna Carta of Women (RA 9710) and other related gender-related laws and policies.

As a research institute under the Department of Science and Technology, ASTI recognizes the importance of gender mainstreaming in S&T to ensure that the benefits of scientific research and technological innovations are accessible to all, regardless of gender.

// GAD MANDATE AND FRAMEWORK

ASTI's GAD program operates under the following legal and policy frameworks:

  • Magna Carta of Women (RA 9710)
  • GAD Code of 2016
  • PCW-NEDA-DBM-DepEd Joint Circular No. 2016-01
  • DOST Gender and Development Framework
